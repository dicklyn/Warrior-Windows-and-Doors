Window Styles for Every Lifestyle

We offer a complete range of window designs to fit any architectural style. Each option balances functionality with aesthetics, ensuring you never have to compromise. Below are our most requested styles, each carefully crafted for performance and beauty:

Window Styles for Every Lifestyle

Sliding Windows

Sliding windows are known for their simplicity and ease of use. They glide horizontally on tracks, making them perfect for wide walls where airflow and sunlight are priorities. Their modern, streamlined appearance makes them ideal for contemporary homes while remaining budget-friendly and durable.

Awning Windows

Hinged at the top, awning windows open outward to allow airflow even during light rain. They are excellent in kitchens and bathrooms, where ventilation is key. Their tight seal also makes them energy-efficient, providing both comfort and savings.

Casement Windows

Casement windows swing outward like a door, operated by a crank. This design allows full top-to-bottom ventilation, unobstructed views, and one of the tightest seals against air leakage. Homeowners appreciate their energy performance and modern elegance.

Picture Windows

Picture windows don’t open, but they offer the best way to showcase outdoor views. Ideal for living rooms and lounges, they bring in maximum sunlight while serving as a design feature that adds spaciousness and style to interiors.

Bay Windows

Bay windows extend outward from your wall, creating a cozy nook inside. Often designed with three panels, they add depth, natural light, and a classic architectural look. Many homeowners use the extra space as a reading corner or seating area.

Egress Windows

Egress windows are safety essentials in basements and bedrooms. They meet building codes by providing a safe emergency exit. Beyond safety, they allow natural light and ventilation in areas that often feel dark or closed-off.

Garden Windows

Garden windows are like mini greenhouses, projecting outward to capture sunlight. Perfect for kitchens, they are excellent for growing herbs, plants, or displaying decorations. They add charm while improving indoor brightness.

Single & Double Hung Windows

Classic and versatile, hung windows feature sashes that slide vertically. Easy to operate and clean, they are a practical choice for many Arizona homeowners. Their traditional style blends well with both modern and historic homes.
